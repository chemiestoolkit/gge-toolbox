The Rift Tournament is a competitive season wrapped around the Rift Raid. While your alliance fights the boss as normal, you now also climb a ladder of six divisions, bank a brand-new currency (Rift Points), clear quests for score, and earn two extra reward tracks on top of the usual boss loot. Do well, rank up, and the prizes get better each season.

It sits on top of the boss fight — it doesn't replace it. You still breach walls and clear the courtyard. The Tournament layers quests, a division leaderboard and the new Rift currencies over the top. New to the rift? Read Rift Raid Basics first.

The six divisions

Every alliance sits in one of six divisions. The higher your division, the more Tournament levels you can reach and the better the reward pool. At the end of each window your alliance is ranked against the others in its sub-division and moves up or down for next time.

Copper → Glass → Bronze → Silver → Gold → Diamond — Diamond (added in this rework) is the new top tier. Promotion/demotion is by placement in your sub-division:

First time in, your alliance is seeded into a starting division before normal ranking begins. Exact promote/demote counts per sub-division are set server-side.

Rift Points — the new currency

The Tournament introduces Rift Points, which replace the old Activity Points. You earn them in battle (breaching walls, clearing courtyards, killing units), and there's now gear that boosts the rate — "+X% Rift Points earned in battle." Rift Points do two jobs: they gate the Boss Defeat Rewards (hit the point threshold on a boss to claim), and they feed your quests.

How you score: the Quest Board

Score comes from quests, not the boss directly. Quests come in three difficulties — bronze, silver and gold — and each one you complete adds points that push your alliance up the Tournament level track.

RuleDetail (official)
Quest ticketsstart with 2; earn +2 for every boss your alliance defeats; buy up to 3 more
Acceptingone quest per ticket; you have 6 hours to finish it once accepted
Rerollingcompletely free, with a 1-hour cooldown per slot

Quest objectives cover the whole toolkit of rift play:

  • Slay a number of the boss's specific units
  • Consume battle/support tools in an attack
  • Gather Rift Points on walls or in the courtyard
  • Finish courtyard attacks within a time limit
  • Open Rift chests and collect event currencies

The two reward tracks

On top of the usual Boss Defeat loot, the Tournament pays out two ways:

TrackHow you get it
Level rewardsEvery time your alliance crosses a point threshold and gains a Tournament level, every eligible member is paid out. This is the reworked replacement for the old Activity Rewards. Higher division = more levels = better payouts.
Ranking / division rewardsGiven to the top alliances in your sub-division at the end of the window, scaled by division. The winning alliance takes the Rift Tournament title.

The prestige gear and cosmetics:

  • Riftsliver Amulet (Commander slot) — additional attack waves in your marches.
  • Riftsliver Loop (Castellan slot) — courtyard, castle-defence and wall-defender bonuses. The Diamond version lasts for months.
  • "Rift Raid Conqueror" coat of arms — a recruitment-speed alliance crest per division; the Diamond crest reaches +1500% recruitment speed.
  • Division Castellan & Commander skins — Bronze / Silver / Gold / Diamond reskins of your castles and marching army (top four divisions only).
  • Premium booster tools — new tools that combine a Rift-Point bonus with wall / gate reduction.
  • Premium Rift Boxes + the Rift Tournament reward set — see the Loot Box overview →
Handy rules change shipped with it: support tools with conditional effects are now only consumed when their effect actually triggers in battle — no more wasting a tool when the condition never fires.
Reward quantities aren't public yet. Which division / level / placement gives how many of each item is delivered by the server at event time and isn't in the client files. The fastest way to pin them down: open the in-game reward hub and division reward panels during a live window and screenshot them.

Quick takeaways

  • Kill bosses — each alliance boss kill hands the whole team +2 quest tickets, so more kills = more score attempts.
  • Quests give points; points raise your alliance level; your level × division decides rewards.
  • Reroll is free (1h/slot) — dump any quest you can't clear in 6 hours and pull a better one.
  • Chase Rift Points in battle: they unlock the Boss Defeat Rewards and feed quests.
  • Climbing divisions is the long game — Diamond unlocks the strongest Riftsliver gear (and that +1500% recruitment crest).
