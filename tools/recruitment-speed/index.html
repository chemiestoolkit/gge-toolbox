⚒️ Recruitment Speed Caps

Stack your recruitment-speed decorations and see how close you are to the game's +4500% recruitment-speed cap and +20% recruitment-cost cap — so you know exactly when you've capped and every extra deco is wasted.

Recruitment decorations

How the caps work: decoration recruitment-speed bonuses are capped at +4500% and recruitment-cost reduction at +20% across all your decos combined — anything past a cap is wasted. The strongest Exalted decos alone can nearly cap speed, so you rarely need many. Caps and deco values come straight from the game data. Buildings, research and feast add recruitment speed separately from this deco cap — see the Maximise Recruitment guide.
