🏆 Rift Tournament Rewards

Both Tournament reward tracks — the ranking (leaderboard) rewards for every division rank by rank, and the full 30-level level-reward track with its point thresholds — decoded straight from the game files. The in-game screen only shows your current division, so this is the full picture, including the tiers your alliance hasn't reached yet. Mechanics live in the Rift Tournament guide.

RankRewards (every eligible member)

Eligibility: contribute at least 250 Rift Tournament Points during the event — and stay in your alliance until it ends (leaving forfeits your ranking rewards). Subdivision winners also receive the Rift Tournament Trophy.

Alliances compete inside a subdivision of their division; placement there decides promotion and demotion each season.

This first run is the Distribution phase: every alliance starts in Copper on one big division leaderboard (ranking rewards are still fought out in random subdivisions). Final placement seeds next season's divisions — per the in-game rules: top 36 → Gold, 37–96 → Silver, 97–456 → Bronze, 457–1,356 → Glass, the rest stay Copper. Diamond isn't seeded directly this run — it's reached by winning a Gold subdivision from season two onward.

One shared 30-level track for the whole event: every time your alliance crosses a point threshold, every eligible member (250+ points contributed) is paid automatically — and the alliance banks +2 quest tickets per level. Your division caps how far the track goes:

🟫 Copper level 5 🔷 Glass level 10 🥉 Bronze level 15 🥈 Silver level 20 🥇 Gold level 25 💎 Diamond level 30
